# Executive Summary — Meridian Inventory Dashboard Modernization

**Prepared for:** Meridian Components, Inc.  
**In Response to:** RFP # MC-2026-0417  
**Date:** May 8, 2026  

## The Opportunity
Meridian Components, Inc. has established itself as a leading distributor of industrial automation parts across its critical hubs in San Francisco, London, and Tokyo. As the company grows, its operations team—led by VP of Operations R. Tanaka—requires a modern, robust, and reliable inventory management dashboard to optimize supply chain flows, control supplier spend, and make data-driven restocking decisions.

The previous vendor left a functional but incomplete system. Key issues include:
- A defective **Reports module** with broken filters, i18n issues, and inconsistent data patterns.
- An absolute absence of **automated test coverage**, which has caused the IT team to halt further deployments.
- No integrated capability to assist the procurement and ops team in **restocking decisions** under specific budget constraints.

Our firm is uniquely positioned to modernize, secure, and expand the Meridian Inventory Dashboard. We understand that this engagement is not just about writing code; it is about restoring operational trust, enabling VP R. Tanaka's team to make confident procurement decisions, and establishing a robust CI/CD and testing framework that satisfies the highest standards of Meridian's IT department.

## Our Solution
We propose a complete, high-velocity remediation and feature expansion plan delivered over a 4-week timeline:
1. **Quality First (R3 & IT Unlocking):** We will immediately establish a robust automated testing suite using **Playwright**. By placing automation and reliability first, we will satisfy IT's requirements from Day 1 and unblock continuous, safe deployments.
2. **Defect Remediation (R1):** We will systematically audit, patch, and verify the existing Reports module—remedying all 8+ reported issues including internationalization gaps (essential for the Tokyo warehouse team) and UI filter synchronization.
3. **Restocking Engine (R2):** We will design and implement a smart restocking interface that recommends purchase orders based on stock levels, historical demand forecasts, and an adjustable budget ceiling.
4. **Architecture Modernization (R4):** We will conduct a thorough current-state architecture review, producing clear developer-facing documentation to ensure the platform remains maintainable for years to come.

## Why Our Firm?
- **Domain Expertise:** Deep experience modernizing legacy supply-chain and distribution dashboards.
- **Narrative & Partnership Alignment:** We recognize that J. Okafor (Director of Procurement) requires absolute timeline and pricing predictability, and R. Tanaka (VP Operations) requires an immediate end to dashboard frustration. We align our delivery milestones directly with these organizational needs.
- **Commitment to Quality:** Rather than treating testing as an afterthought, we treat it as our core deployment driver.
